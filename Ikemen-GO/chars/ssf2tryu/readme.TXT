==============Movelist===============

Here's how moves will be notated:

LP - Weak punch
MP - Medium punch
HP - Strong punch
LK - Weak kick
MK - Medium kick
HK - Strong kick
P - Any punch
K - Any kick
==============Special Moves==============
*Hadouken - D, DF, F + P
*Shoryuken - F, D, DF + P
*Tatsumaki Senpuu Kyaku - D, DB, B + K (can be done in the air)
==============The Super Combo==============
*Shinkuu Hadouken - Hold B, F, B, F, K
==============Play as Old Ryu==============
To play as Old Ryu, press Start when selecting him.
